## Executive Summary

This project developed a Power BI dashboard to analyze financial sales performance across customer segments, products, countries, and discount categories. The dashboard provides an interactive view of key metrics, including revenue, profit, sales trends, and profitability, helping users monitor business performance and identify insights for decision-making.

<img width="1326" height="717" alt="Dashboard" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/3e70feb4-18f4-4df0-b28f-39c97d946770" />

### Key Trends & Insights

- The company generated **$127.93M in revenue** and **$16.89M in profit**, resulting in an overall **13.21% profit margin**.

- Revenue increased by approximately **164%** from September 2023 to December 2024, with noticeable sales spikes during Q4 periods.

- The **Government segment** generated the highest revenue at **$56.40M**, while **Channel Partners** achieved the highest profit margin at **68.05%**.

- The **Enterprise segment** generated over **$21M in revenue** but still recorded a **-2.92% profit margin**, showing profitability issues despite strong sales performance.

- **Paseo** was the top-performing product, generating **$36M in revenue**, while High and Medium discount bands produced the highest sales volumes.

## Recommendations

Based on the findings from the dashboard, the following recommendations were identified:

- **Review pricing and discount strategies for the Enterprise segment** because the segment generated high sales revenue but still showed negative profitability.

- **Expand Channel Partner programs** since this segment achieved the highest profit margin and showed strong profitability potential.

- **Increase focus on high-performing products such as Paseo** because it consistently generated the highest revenue among all products.

- **Monitor discount usage regularly** because higher discount levels helped increase sales volume but may reduce overall profitability if used too often.

## Project Overview

This project developed a Power BI dashboard to analyze financial sales performance using raw sales data. It includes interactive insights into revenue, profit, sales trends, customer segments, product performance, and discount impact. The data was prepared and transformed in Excel and Power BI to create a clear and interactive report for monitoring business performance.

Insights and recommendations are provided on the following key areas:

- **Revenue & Profitability Analysis:** Review of revenue growth, profit performance, profit margins, and sales trends over time.

- **Customer Segment Performance:** Comparison of revenue and profitability across different customer segments to identify strong and weak-performing areas.

- **Country Performance:** Comparison of revenue and profitability across countries to identify stronger-performing markets.

- **Product & Discount Performance:** Review of product revenue performance and the impact of discount levels on sales volume and profitability.

## Data Structure & Initial Checks

The dataset used for this project contains financial sales transaction data related to revenue, profit, products, customer segments, discounts, and sales dates. The Power BI data model includes three main tables used for reporting and dashboard analysis.

### Tables Used

| Table Name | Description |
|---|---|
| Financials | Main table containing sales transaction data such as revenue, profit, units sold, discounts, products, and customer segments |
| dCalendar | Date table used for monthly and yearly sales trend analysis |
| _Measures | Dedicated table containing DAX calculations and dashboard metrics |
